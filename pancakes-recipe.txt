Ingredients:
 - 2 cups flour
 - 2 tablespoons sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 tablespoons baking powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 3 small eggs (or 2 big eggs)
 - 1/4 cup melted butter
 - 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
 - milk to texture (1 to 2 cups)

Directions:
1. Mix ingredients
2. Heat pan with low constant heat
3. Cook and eat delicious pancakes
